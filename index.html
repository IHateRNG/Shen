Ki Barrier

COOLDOWN: 11

INATO: After completing an ability's effects, Shen grants himself a shield for 47 - 120 (based on level) (+ 13% bonus health) for 2.5 seconds. If Shen affects at least one other champion with an ability, Ki Barrier's cooldown is reduced by 4 - 8 (based on level) seconds.

Shen manifests a Spirit Blade that he can control wit Twilight Assault and Spirit's Refuge.
